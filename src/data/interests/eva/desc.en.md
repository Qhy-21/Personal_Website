# Neon Genesis Evangelion

*EVA*

Hideaki Anno wrapped a philosophical core about loneliness, existence, communication barriers, and self-identity inside giant robot vs. Angel battles.

The AT Field is both a physical shield and the invisible "wall of the heart" between people.

Every watch, I see myself in different characters: Shinji's avoidance, Asuka's pride, Rei's identity confusion, Misato's contradictions and tenderness.
