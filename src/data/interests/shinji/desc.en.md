# Shinji Ikari

EVA Unit-01 pilot, key to the Human Instrumentality Project, and — the most controversial protagonist in anime history.

He has none of the courage and resolve of a typical shounen hero.

He runs away, he cries, he says "I don't want to pilot the Eva" before every battle. Yet every single time, he gets in the cockpit.

Not because he's brave, but because he's afraid — afraid of being abandoned, afraid of letting others down, afraid of the emptiness that comes from doing nothing.

Shinji's very existence is a deconstruction of what it means to be a "hero."
